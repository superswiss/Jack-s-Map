🕵️ What is this?

The Mission

This is a spy-themed scavenger hunt designed for the Howell Creek District neighborhood. Kids play as secret agents for the Agency of Howell Sneak, solving puzzles and visiting real locations to stop a rogue AI threat — all guided by their AI handler, STEVE.

How it works

Each stage presents a mission from STEVE. Some stages ask you to solve a riddle, some require you to physically go to a location (verified by GPS), and others involve scanning a QR code on a hidden object. Completing each stage unlocks the next.

Location & Camera access

This app requests permission to use your phone's location (to verify when you've reached a mission site) and camera (to scan QR codes on hidden objects). These permissions are only used in the moment — no data is sent anywhere or stored outside this device.

Privacy

No data is collected, transmitted, or shared. All game progress is stored only in this browser on this device. Nothing leaves your phone.

Save your progress

Use the Dossier (📋) to export a progress code you can paste on another device, or to restore progress if you switch phones mid-mission.

For parents

The GM (Game Master) section of the Dossier is protected by a 4-digit passcode. It lets you skip stages, reveal hints, or reset the hunt. The passcode hint is in the Dossier screen.
