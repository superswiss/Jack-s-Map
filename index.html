The Adventure

This is a family scavenger hunt app with two missions: a spy thriller in the Howell Creek District neighborhood, and a pirate treasure hunt at Disney's Vero Beach Resort. Each mission leads you to real locations where you solve clues to find the next one.

How it works

Each stage shows you a clue and a map. Make your way to the location, then answer the on-site question to unlock the next clue. The map shows all locations — but locked ones stay mysterious until you reach them.

Location & Camera access

This app requests your phone's location to confirm when you've reached a clue site, and your camera to scan QR codes in the spy mission. Permissions are used only in the moment — nothing is sent anywhere or stored outside this device.

Privacy

No data is collected, transmitted, or shared. All progress is stored only in this browser on this device.

Save your progress

Use the Dossier (📋) to export a progress code you can paste on another device, or restore if you switch phones.

For parents

The GM section of the Dossier is passcode-protected. It lets you skip stages or reset the hunt. The passcode hint is shown there.
